## Wire Color Coding (Recommended)

```
Power:
  Red    = Positive (+)
  Black  = Ground (GND)
  Yellow = 12V rail
  Orange = 7.4V rail (servo)

Signals:
  Blue   = I2C SDA
  Purple = I2C SCL
  Green  = UART TX
  White  = UART RX
  Gray   = PWM signals
  Brown  = Encoder signals

Motors:
  Red/Black pairs per motor
  Label with leg name (FL, FR, BL, BR)
```

## Quick Reference: Wire Gauges

```
AWG to mm² Conversion:
======================

14 AWG = 2.08 mm² (battery, high current)
16 AWG = 1.31 mm² (motors, power)
18 AWG = 0.82 mm² (medium current)
20 AWG = 0.52 mm² (low current)
22 AWG = 0.33 mm² (signals, I2C)
24 AWG = 0.20 mm² (fine signals)

Current Capacity (approximate):
===============================

14 AWG: up to 15A
16 AWG: up to 10A
18 AWG: up to 7A
20 AWG: up to 5A
22 AWG: up to 3A
24 AWG: up to 2A
```
